How to play

  • Move the scooter left/right (← → or A/D, or drag/touch) to catch yellow cans.
  • Catching cans earns points and fills the Well. Hazards reduce score and reset combo.
  • Reach 100% Well Progress before time runs out to win. You can still click items if you prefer.

Ready to help fill the well?

Catch cans, avoid hazards, and hit milestones to learn impact facts.
