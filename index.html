How to play

  • Move left/right (← → or A/D, or drag/touch) to catch yellow cans.
  • Catching cans earns points and fills the Well; hazards reduce score and progress.
  • Reach the goal % before time runs out to win!

Ready to help fill the well?

Catch cans, avoid hazards, and hit milestones to learn impact facts.

About charity: water

charity: water is a nonprofit organization bringing clean and safe water to people around the world. 100% of public donations go directly to funding water projects.
